Events

The SA Greys Board meets on the second Sunday of select months: January, February, April, July, September, October, November and December. The meeting is open to all, so if you have the time and the inclination, you can sit in on a board meeting and watch the Board members make decisions and plans for the operation of the organization.

MotoSonora Meet up

Southern Arizona Greyhound Adoption and MotoSonora Brewing are partnering for a greyhound meetup on Saturday, March 23 from 2 to 4 p.m.

Come on down and support an award-winning locally-owned brewery, enjoy the outdoor patio and Pizza Sonora food truck. Meet and chat with other adopters. Bring a hound mat. Non-alcoholic beverages are available. Hockey tickets will also be for sale for only $20 (reg $37) for the April event (details below). See you there. 1015 S. Park Ave, Tucson 85719. Free parking in back. (No small dogs, please.)

Tucson Roadrunners Charity Partner of the Night event

Roadrunners Hockey Hosts the Hounds in Tucson on Friday, April 19 at 7 p.m. Tickets are $20 and the proceeds go to SA Greys. Buy tickets here.
